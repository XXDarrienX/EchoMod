Unbidden Warlock - Increased ship speed to 25% from 15%

###Tradition Changes###

##Industry##
Industry Blueprints - Replaced the Research Options for the industry buildings. Replaced with a Factor 5 modifier for getting the Alloy and CG building techs and a -15% Metallurgist Upkeep and -15% Artisan Upkeep for Normal Empires and Rogue Servitors. If your not a Gestalt and not a Rogue Servitor, the bonus is -30% for Metallurgist alone to balance it out.
Multi Functional Cargobay - Removed the set Resource Storage and replaced it with +5k Resource Storage on Planetary Silos and Starbase Silos. 
Ultra-efficient Assemblies - Removed the job from the buildings, replaced with +25% Industrial District Buildspeed and -25% Industrial District Build Cost.

##Kleptocracy##
Truce amongsts Rogues - No longer piracy immune. +25 Starbase Trade Protection, +25% Ship Piracy Suppression, +25% Damage vs Pirates
Piracy Culture - Reduced the ship speed bonus to 10% instead of +15%

##Mysticism##
Sanctum Buildings(Basic Ones) - All changes to be +10% to the resource output of the resource their jobs generate. Nothing else changed
Sanctum of Keys - Normal Empire - +15% Worker Output, +30% Habitability, Hive Empire - Simple Drone Output +15%, +30% Habitability, Machine Empiure - +15% Simple Drone Output, +5% Amenities.
Sanctum of Vision - Hive Empire - +3 Synapse Drones, Machine Empire - 1 Coordinator Drone, 3 Evaluator Drones

##Malice##
Adoption - +2 Encryption and +2 Decryption

###Defines Changes###
Orbital Bombardment Fleet Size Cap Increased to 300 from 200

##Ship Components##
Sensors - Sensor Technology for ships has a max sensor range of 3 at max tech. In exchange, sensors will increase Ship Interstellar Speed at +5% per module tech level starting at +10%. Sensors will also grant a % of a tracking bonus instead of a flat number at +5% per level starting at +10%.
Thrusters - All thrusters were adjusted to go increments of +10% per level, The highest speed thruster has a bonus of 60% vs 150% it used to have.
Pulsed Plasma Afterburners - Can not be installed on anything larger than a Destroyer, except for the StrikeCruiser this is to prevent high evasion ships that should not have high evasion.

##Starbase Buildings##
Sensor Jammer Aura Building - Enemy ship sensors are reduced to 0

increasing duration of the penatlies
